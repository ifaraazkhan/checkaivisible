# Phase 0 Outbound Scripts

> **Goal:** validate the infrastructure thesis BEFORE building more.
> **Window:** 14 days from start.
> **Target signal:** 3+ platform sales conversations + 5+ agency early-access signups + 1+ multi-location group asking for a quote.
> **Tools:** LinkedIn Sales Navigator (free trial), Hunter.io (free tier), Apollo (free tier), Loom for demo videos.

---

## Outreach List Sizing

| Audience | Volume | Channel | Reply target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Vertical SaaS PMs | 20 | LinkedIn DM | 4 replies (20%) → 3 calls |
| Local SEO agencies | 30 | LinkedIn DM | 9 replies (30%) → 5 signups |
| Multi-location groups (5+ sites) | 50 | Cold email | 5 replies (10%) → 1 paid pilot ask |
| **Total touches** | **100** | | |

If you can't hit 100 touches in 14 days, the bottleneck is execution, not the idea. Reduce surface area, not effort.

## 1. Vertical SaaS Platform PMs (Layer 1 — Data API)

**Target accounts (start here):**
- Toast (restaurant POS) — search "Product Manager" + "Toast"
- Square Local Services
- OpenTable, Resy, SevenRooms
- Vagaro, Boulevard, Squire, Mindbody (beauty/wellness)
- Housecall Pro, Jobber, ServiceTitan (home services)
- Cliniko, Practice Better (clinics)
- Toast Local, DoorDash Marketplace (food delivery)

**Title filters:** Senior PM, Group PM, Head of Merchant Tools, Head of Local SEO, Director of Product (Merchant Experience).

### LinkedIn DM v1 — Cold

> Subject (intro request): Quick question about AI visibility for {{Company}} merchants
>
> Hi {{firstName}} —
>
> 45% of US consumers now use ChatGPT or Gemini to find local businesses (BrightLocal 2026). Most {{Company}} merchants have no idea whether they show up in those responses.
>
> I built an engine that scores any local business on AI visibility across ChatGPT and Gemini — works at the (city × category × prompt) level so it's cheap enough to run at platform scale.
>
> I ran a sample report on 5 of your highest-traffic {{vertical}} merchants. Want me to send it over? No pitch — just the report. If it's useful we can talk about an API.
>
> — {{yourName}}

**Variant for vertical context:** swap `{{vertical}}` with "restaurants" / "salons" / "dental practices" etc.

**Hard rule:** never send without the actual sample report already prepared. The hook is the artifact.

### LinkedIn DM v2 — Follow-up after 5 business days (if no reply)

> Hi {{firstName}} — bumping this. I'll keep it short: here's the sample report for {{Company}} merchants → {{Notion link}}
>
> Took me ~30 min to produce. If you wanted this for 1,000 merchants on a weekly cadence, that's the API. Worth a 15-min call?

### Email fallback (if LinkedIn doesn't reply in 10 days)

> Subject: Sample AI visibility report — {{merchant 1}}, {{merchant 2}}, {{merchant 3}}
>
> {{firstName}},
>
> I sent a LinkedIn note last week with a sample AI visibility report for {{Company}} merchants — wanted to put it directly in your inbox in case LinkedIn buried it.
>
> Report (Notion, 5 merchants, 2 platforms, 20 prompts each):
> {{Notion link}}
>
> The interesting line is the gap: {{X}}/5 of your sampled merchants are invisible on ChatGPT for their primary category query. That's the line that gets executive attention.
>
> If this is interesting, I can show you the engine in a 15-min Loom or live call. If not, no follow-up.
>
> — {{yourName}}
> checkaivisible.com

### Discovery call agenda (15 min)

1. (2 min) "Tell me how you currently think about AI traffic for your merchants."
2. (5 min) Demo: score, breakdown, competitor view, leaderboard.
3. (3 min) "If this lived in your merchant dashboard, who owns it on your side?"
4. (3 min) Pricing test: "Other platforms we're in conversations with pay $5–25K/mo depending on volume. Is that in the realm of feasible for {{Company}}?"
5. (2 min) Next step: design-partner agreement, 60-day pilot, free.

## 2. Local SEO Agencies (Layer 2 — Agency Dashboard)

**Target accounts:** boutique agencies with 5–50 local-business clients. Search LinkedIn:
- Title: "Owner" OR "Founder" OR "Director" at companies with "local SEO" or "digital marketing" in the description
- Geo: US-based
- Company size: 2–50 employees

**Communities to mine:**
- r/SEO, r/bigSEO (look at recent posters)
- Local Search Forum (Whitespark community)
- The SEO Community Slack
- BrightLocal community
- LocalU attendees list

### LinkedIn DM v1 — Cold

> Hi {{firstName}} —
>
> Building a tool for agencies like yours: AI visibility scoring for client locations across ChatGPT and Gemini. Multi-client dashboard, monthly auto-refresh, white-label PDF.
>
> Looking for 10 agencies for free early access in exchange for ~30 min of feedback. Saw {{specific thing about their agency}} and thought you'd have good signal on whether this is useful.
>
> Interested? I'll send a Loom demo and access link.

### Loom follow-up (after they say yes)

3-minute Loom covering:
1. (30s) Problem framing: "your clients are asking about AI traffic, you don't have a tool for it yet"
2. (90s) Live audit demo with a real local business
3. (45s) Multi-client view mock + white-label PDF mock
4. (15s) CTA: "Reply with 3 client URLs and I'll run them this week"

### Discovery call (20 min, after Loom)

1. (5 min) "Walk me through how you currently report on local SEO performance to clients."
2. (5 min) Live demo on their actual clients' URLs.
3. (5 min) Pricing test: "Pricing will be $99 / $299 / $999 by client count. Where would your agency fit?"
4. (5 min) Next steps: free 60-day pilot, asks for written feedback at day 30 and day 60.

## 4. Personal-Network Outreach (warm)

Don't skip this. The warm intro is worth 10 cold DMs.

### Asks you should send by end of week 1

| Person type | Ask |
|---|---|
| Any friend at a vertical SaaS | "Intro to your PM owning merchant tools/local SEO?" |
| Any friend running an agency | "30 min to look at this tool? I'll give you free access for life if you give me real feedback." |
| Any friend on the customer side (restaurant owner, dentist, etc.) | "Can I run your business through this and walk you through the report?" |
| Anyone in local SEO Twitter/X | "Mind sharing a screenshot of your business's score?" — turns into UGC |

## What Counts as a Real Signal

### Strong signal (green light)
- Platform PM books a 2nd meeting with their boss/team
- Agency emails: "When can I have my clients in this?"
- Multi-location operator asks: "What does this cost monthly for all our locations?"
- Anyone sends an unsolicited screenshot of the tool to their network

### Weak signal (yellow)
- "Looks cool, keep me posted"
- "We have a guy who handles SEO" (deflection)
- Sign up for early access but never log in
- Likes / comments on a LinkedIn post but no DM

### Negative signal (red)
- "We already use BrightLocal/Yext for this" → push: "this is separate from local pack — ChatGPT/Gemini don't use GBP for ChatGPT specifically. Show me a screenshot of where they cover that today."
- "Interesting but not a priority this quarter"
- No replies at all from 50+ touches

## Tracking

Spreadsheet (Airtable or Google Sheet, ~10 min to set up). Columns:

| Date | Audience | Name | Company | Title | Channel | Touch # | Response | Stage | Next action | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Stages: `sent → opened → replied → call_booked → call_done → pilot → paid → dead`

Review weekly. If reply rate is below 10% across all three audiences after week 1, the message is wrong, not the market.

## Sample Report Template (the artifact you need to build first)

Before any outreach, build ONE Notion page per audience that you can clone in 5 minutes per prospect:

### For platform PMs (sample-report-platform.md)
- Headline: "{{Company}} merchants — AI visibility snapshot (5 locations)"
- For each merchant: score, ChatGPT mentions / 20 prompts, Gemini mentions / 20 prompts, top competitor recommended instead, 3 most cited sources
- Aggregate: average score, % invisible, top citation sources across the cohort
- Closing slide: "If you want this for 10,000 merchants on a weekly cadence: API exists. Let's talk."

### For agencies (sample-report-agency.md)
- Pick one of their clients (publicly visible from their case studies page)
- Run the audit, screenshot the entire results page
- Closing: "This is the report. Want multi-client view + monthly auto-refresh? Free 60-day access."

### For multi-location operators (sample-report-multiloc.md)
- 5 of their locations side-by-side in a table
- Single insight: variation between best and worst location
- Closing: "Want all {{N}} locations + monthly tracking? 15 min to scope."

**Build all three template Notion pages before sending a single DM.** They are the entire reason this campaign works.

## Daily Cadence (14 days)

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Build 3 Notion templates. Build outreach spreadsheet. |
| 2 | Hand-run 5 platform sample reports. List 20 PMs. |
| 3 | Send 10 platform DMs. Hand-run 5 agency reports. List 30 agencies. |
| 4 | Send 10 more platform DMs + 15 agency DMs. |
| 5 | Send 15 agency DMs. Hand-run 5 multi-loc reports. List 50 operators. |
| 6 | Send 25 multi-loc emails. |
| 7 | First batch of follow-ups (anyone who hasn't replied in 5 business days). |
| 8 | Send remaining 25 multi-loc emails. Take any inbound calls. |
| 9–10 | Calls + Loom demos. |
| 11 | Round 2 follow-ups. |
| 12 | Calls + Loom demos. |
| 13 | Final follow-ups. Personal network asks. |
| 14 | Tally signals. Make the build-vs-pivot decision. |

---

## Decision at Day 14

| Signal count | Decision |
|---|---|
| 3+ platform calls AND 5+ agency signups | **Build the full v1 plan + start API design** |
| 0–2 platform calls AND 5+ agency signups | **Build v1 plan focused on agency tier, defer API to month 4** |
| 0 platform AND 0 agency AND 0 multi-loc | **Don't build. Kill or pivot the positioning.** |
| Mixed: some interest but no payment intent | **Extend 14 more days with stronger artifacts (Loom demos, fake-door pricing).** |
